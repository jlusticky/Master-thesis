\chapter{Quagga}
Building quagga with patch:
\\
libtoolize, aclocal, autoconf, autoheader, automake --add-missing,
./configure --disable-doc --enable-user=root --enable-group=root --enable-vty-group=root --enable-vtysh
make, make install
